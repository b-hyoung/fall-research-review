# Product Context

## register
product

## Users
- Primary: Team MajuBom members (5 university students in capstone design)
- Secondary: Supervising professor reviewing research progress
- Tertiary: External evaluators at capstone presentations

## Product Purpose
A multi-page web presentation system for a fall prediction research project targeting dementia patients. Functions as an interactive research review and capstone presentation tool. Pages include paper surveys, positioning analysis, prediction model details, user flow scenarios, integrated presentation slides, and reference listings.

## Brand & Tone
- Academic yet approachable: professional enough for professor review, clean enough for presentation
- Data-driven: statistics, citations, and evidence are front and center
- Korean-first: all content in Korean, UI labels in Korean

## Anti-references
- Overly corporate SaaS dashboards
- Dark hacker aesthetic
- Cluttered academic paper layouts with no visual hierarchy

## Strategic Principles
- Clarity over decoration: every visual element serves comprehension
- Consistency across pages: shared nav, color system, spacing rhythm
- Mobile-friendly: must work for quick reference on phones during presentations
- Light theme (main pages): soft grays, clean whites, accent blue (#3563e9)
- Information density balanced with whitespace
